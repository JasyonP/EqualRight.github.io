About Us

This group aims to raise awareness about gender equality and help people understand the importance of treating everyone fairly, regardless of gender. The campaign will focus on educating students and faculty about gender sensitivity and how it can create a more inclusive and respectful community.

Objectives

  • 🎓 Educate people about gender sensitivity and its importance.
  • 💪 Empower individuals to stand up against gender discrimination.
  • 🗣️ Encourage open discussions about gender issues and solutions.
  • 🌍 Break gender stereotypes and encourage inclusivity.

Target Audience

This campaign will focus on college students and faculty members.

🌟 Activities 🌟

📢

Seminar

Guest speakers from advocacy groups and educators will discuss gender equality.

📖

Storytelling Session

Individuals share real-life experiences about gender equality and challenges.

❓

Trivia Game

A simple quiz to test and reinforce knowledge on gender equality.

💬

Scenario-Based Discussions

Small groups analyze real-life situations related to gender bias.

🤔

Group Reflection

A closing activity where participants reflect on what they learned.

📅 Campaign Events

Week 1: Preparation & Promotion

• Finalize event details, secure guest speakers, and prepare materials.

• Begin promoting the campaign through digital posters and social media.

• Launch the campaign website with initial content.

Week 2: Awareness

• Host the Seminar with guest speakers to introduce gender equality and its challenges.

• Allow time for Q&A and open discussions with participants.

Week 3: Participation & Discussion

• Conduct the Storytelling Session, where chosen individuals share experiences.

• Host the Trivia Game to test and reinforce knowledge on gender equality.

• Facilitate Scenario-Based Discussions, allowing participants to analyze gender-related issues.

Week 4: Reflection & Impact

• Conduct the Group Reflection session to gather insights and personal takeaways.

• Encourage participants to share their commitments to gender equality.

• Post-event evaluation: gather feedback and measure the campaign’s impact.

📊 Monitoring & Evaluation

The evaluation results are based on previous events and the total number of respondents. The gathered feedback highlights the project's effectiveness in promoting gender equality and provides insights for enhancing future activities. By analyzing participant responses, we can identify key areas of improvement and develop more impactful initiatives.

  • 🔹85% of participants found the seminar informative and engaging.
  • 🔹78% actively participated in the storytelling session.
  • 🔹90% enjoyed the trivia game and felt it reinforced their knowledge.
  • 🔹70% found scenario-based discussions helpful in understanding gender bias.
  • 🔹80% reflected positively on the group reflection session and shared meaningful insights.
